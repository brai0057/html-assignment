HTML Tags I Learned

<p>
Defines a paragraph.
<h1> to <h6>
Headings with different levels of importance.
<img>
Embeds an image into the page.
<a>
Creates a hyperlink to another page or resource.
<ul> and <li>
Creates an unordered list with list items.
